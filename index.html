Why you need a TMS

In the complex  world of logistics having a seamless operations is essential to stay competitive in whatever industry you are in. One small error ca result in operational inefficiencies and costly delays. To stay competitive , these challenges need to be eliminated.

What we have

Our transport Management System grants real-time operational visibility and helps you manage your overall logistics remotely in a single platform, it enables you to automate the processes such Shipment  Visibility, Vehicle Optimization, Shipment Assignment, Yard and Dispatch Management, and generation of reports.

Features

Explore our capabilities

Fleet Management

Manage and monitor your fleet/3PL’s in a single view

Vehicle Optimization 

Simplify assigning of shipments and maximize vehicle capacity

Yard and Dispatch Management

Anticipate yard movement and plan driver appointments in a timely manner.

Track and Trace

Real-time tracking of shipments and events such as loading, unloading, arrival, and delivery completion

Reports and Analytics

Genereate dashboards and downloadable KPI reports such as Call time compliance, On-time Delivery, Delivery in Full

Drivers App

Provides navigation support for drivers. This app is also used to log driver activities and enables capturing of Electonic Proof of Deliveries (ePod)

Document Management System

Provides a central repository for important content such as digitally created files; or paper documents that have been scanned for filing and storage.

Plate Number Recognition

Plate recognition utilizes an optical character recognition (OCR) capability and motion sensor to read vehicle license plates and create incoming and outgoing reports on dwell time.
